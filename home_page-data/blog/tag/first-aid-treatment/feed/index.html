]]> Yearly, a large number of people are hurt and injured across the globe. Wikihow stated that about 20-50 million people get hurt across the globe. One common happening causing this is a car accident – specifically in consideration of the environmental surroundings. Moreover, it is one of the things that humans experience in our daily lives. Notably, a car accident is a tragic experience, a moment that is filled with shock. Nevertheless, does that stop us from going about our businesses? Of course, no. While handling your daily activities, what will you do if you encounter someone that needs your attention? No idea, but what I do know is that you have a lot to give, provided that you know the first aid treatment for a car accident victim.

First aid treatment for a car accident victim

This, however, takes us sliding to the first aid treatment for a car accident victim. Quickly, we will brief details, containing a broad ideology on the process it takes. Breaking it down into two parts, we have:

  • Securing the scene of the accident
  • Provide the appropriate aid

 

1: Securing the scene of the accident

Here are the things to do when in a rescue situation:

Roles of School Nurses in healthcare development

Pull by the roadside of the scene

Often when you encounter an accident with an injured victim, you have to pull by the roadside. Considerably, your next motive should be barricading the scene – simply by putting a clear signal for others to see.

Stay calm

Note; you shouldn’t operate on a victim with panic in you. More importantly, try to evacuate any signal of panic around the scene. You never know; all of that might cause an effect on the victim who is still struggling for survival.

Quickly access the vicinity

If at all, you have experience in giving attention, you should first look around you. Check for detailed facts before operating. You shouldn’t take all the time doing this, but quickly, you can access the environments. Why is this needed? For aiding the process by which the victim gets a response. For example, you can check if there was a fire outbreak or the presence of children in the scene. All these require you to respond immediately. You might as well want to secure yourself if it’s a danger zone.

Call emergency services

After accessing the scene, pick your phone and contact the emergency services. Also, tell others to do the same. Who knows? They might observe something that may be of great help.

Make sure there is a clear signal securing the scene

Ensure that the scene is surrounded by barricades, so anyone approaching gets an idea of the incident. Others may see the need to stop by and support the victim.

Having looked at the helpful things we can do for an accident victim, let’s dive into the provision of first aid attention.

 

2: Providing the appropriate aid

Briefly, the necessities for this aspect are as follows:

 

  • Check for any signal of danger.
  • Surely, you should ask the victim if he or she needs assistance before doing so, and that’s if you still notice some consciousness. Not yielding to a person’s wish can attract legal actions.
  • Avoid imposing any motion on the victim.
  • Check the airway to see if the person is still breathing. If not, do CPR.
  • Provide aid only if it’s a life-threatening issue.
  • Keep the person comfortable to relieve from shock.
  • Hand over to the emergency services.

 

First aid treatment for a car accident victim is not something complex to do. It just takes a gradual and appropriate process. Remember it involves both the victim and the scene of the incident.
